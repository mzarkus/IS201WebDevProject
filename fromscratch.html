Career Achievements

  1. Junior
    • 2013 CIK Stars of Karting Champion.
    • 2014 South Australian Junior Championship winner.
    • 2014 Victorian State Junior Championship winner.
  2. Formula 4-2
    • 2017 British F4 vice-champion.
    • 2019 Formula Renault Eurocup Champion.
    • 2020 FIA Formula 3 Champion.
    • 2021 FIA Formula 2 Champion.
    • 2021 FIA Rookie of the Year.
    • First driver to win Formula Renault, Formula 3, and Formula 2 titles in successive seasons.
    • First Formula 2 rookie in years to win the championship.
  3. Formula 1
    • 2023 first Formula 1 podium at the Japanese Grand Prix.
    • 2023 first Formula 1 sprint win at the Qatar Grand Prix.
    • 2024 first Formula 1 Grand Prix win at the Hungarian Grand Prix.
    • 2024 Grand Prix win at the Azerbaijan Grand Prix.
    • Helped McLaren win the Constructors' Championship in 2024.
